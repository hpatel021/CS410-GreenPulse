Societal Problem

With the climate being in as terrible of a state that it is currently, making active choices to lessen our effect on the environment is becoming more and more important each day. The lack of knowledge of what options are more eco-friendly to the general population is the issue. A large amount of society wants to be more eco-conscious. Recent surveys have observed that over 85% of participants have made changes in the last 5 years to their purchase habits (Global Sustainability Study 2021: Initial insights).

Our Solution

GreenPulse is a smartphone application designed to facilitate sustainable living effortlessly. By allowing users to scan products and access real-time trustworthy information on their environmental impact, the app empowers individuals to make informed choices, reduce their carbon footprint, and actively participate in the eco-conscious movement. GreenPulse provides a convenient tool for promoting sustainability in everyday life.
